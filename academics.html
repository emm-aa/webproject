Academics

At Ntare School, we provide a rigorous and diverse academic program designed to challenge and inspire our students. Rooted in our mission to produce God-fearing citizens ready for leadership in Uganda, our curriculum spans Ordinary (O’) and Advanced (A’) Levels, offering a broad range of subjects in sciences, arts, and vocational studies. With a legacy of academic excellence, Ntare prepares students for national examinations (UCE and UACE) and future success in higher education and beyond.

Students undertake a minimum of 8 and up to 10 subjects, preparing for the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE). The curriculum includes core subjects mandated by the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) and optional subjects tailored to student interests. Core Subjects: English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Geography, History. Optional Subjects: Include languages (e.g., Kiswahili, French), vocational subjects (e.g., Commerce, Fine Art), and additional sciences or humanities.

Students specialize in 3 principal subjects and 2 subsidiary subjects, culminating in the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE). Combinations are offered in Sciences (e.g., PCM, BCM) and Arts (e.g., HEG, LEG), with subsidiary options like General Paper and ICT. This prepares students for university and professional careers.
